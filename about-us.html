About Gyanshree

"Knowledge is enlightenment; enlightenment is prosperity."

Established in 2013, under the aegis of a leading Business House, Gyanshree School, Noida is a CBSE affiliated, progressive K-12 school. Within a short span, this International School Award (British Council) accredited school has earned a reputation as one of NCR’s high potential Senior Secondary Schools.

Gyanshree forges ahead with its vision of preparing students to encounter a rapidly changing world by equipping them with critical thinking skills, a global perspective, and respect for core values. It is committed to a curriculum that is both rigorous and balanced, focusing on the intellectual, social, and emotional development of every student.

The hallmark of Gyanshree School is the special "Indianness" curriculum, designed to connect to real life and affect the lives of our students positively. This unique approach ensures that while our students are globally competitive, they remain deeply rooted in the cultural and moral fabric of India.

At Gyanshree, we believe that education is more than just academic achievement; it is about the holistic development of an individual. Our state-of-the-art infrastructure, combined with a dedicated team of educators, creates an environment where students are encouraged to explore their passions and achieve their full potential.

Our endeavor is to provide an environment that is conducive to learning and one that encourages students to be lifelong learners.

We invite you to explore Gyanshree School and witness how we are shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
